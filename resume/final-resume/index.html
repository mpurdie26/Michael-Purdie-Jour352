About Me
Michael Purdie is a recent graduate from the University of Maryland, College Park and data journalist. At UMD, Purdie received a B.A. Journalism from The Philip Merrill College of Journalism and a Women's, Gender, and Sexuality Studies Certificate from The Harriet Tubman Department of Women’s Studies.
He previously worked on two projects for the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism -- one of which was published by the Associated Press and The Washington Post -- and interned at the College Park Here & Now.
